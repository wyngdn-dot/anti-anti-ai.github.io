the problem

what artists are
up against

Three real threats that every creator faces in the AI era — and almost no tool is built to defend against them.

THREAT 01
Unwanted Training & Scraping
Models are trained on copyrighted work without consent. Your catalog, your voice, your sound — silently consumed as fuel for someone else's system. U.S. copyright guidance now flags this as potentially infringing. It keeps happening anyway.
THREAT 02
Look-Alike Outputs
When AI outputs mirror training data too closely, they compete with and dilute the original work's market. Style, voice, and sound are assets. Without protection, they become commons.
THREAT 03
No Provenance Trail
Once art is copied and reshared, proving origin becomes nearly impossible. Without a private signed record, you are arguing from memory against systems that never forget.
the answer

three pillars.
one fortress.

anti-anti is built around the three things every artist needs in the AI era — a sovereign place, a controlled gate, and an unbreakable record.

PILLAR 01
Sovereign Archive
Masters, stems, and artwork live locally — snapshotted and versioned on your own hardware. No external AI or cloud platform can touch, delete, or train on them without your explicit approval. Ever.
PILLAR 02
Front-Door Policy Engine
Anything leaving the system passes through antiforge first. It tags, watermarks, or strips sensitive pieces according to rules you set. Nothing exits unguarded.
PILLAR 03
Provenance Logger
Every export and share writes a signed log entry: time, destination, what was sent. A private paper trail that belongs only to you — built before you ever need it, ready if you ever do.
the vault

your IP.
locked down.

Six cloud platforms charge monthly fees to protect what you already own. Vault does it locally — no upload, no subscription, no platform in the middle.

VAULT // 01
Trusted Proof Bundle
On every export, a tamper-evident ZIP is generated: SHA-256 file hash, provenance excerpt, and a signed README. Labels and lawyers can verify ownership without running a2 or touching your vault.
VAULT // 02
Local Watermark Presets
Imperceptible markers embedded into exported files entirely offline. Three presets: subtle mastering chain, high-frequency marker, metadata embed. Every watermark logged to the Provenance Ledger with timestamp and parameters.
VAULT // 03
Opt-In AI Registry Hooks
Declare "Do Not Train" per asset. Flag stored locally alongside the file hash. Exportable as CSV or JSON for SoundExchange and emerging AI registries. No cloud dependency — you submit when and if you choose.
VAULT // 04
Forensic Provenance Ledger
Append-only signed log of every export: timestamp, destination, file hash, watermark applied, export policy in effect. Stored locally in SQLite. Exportable as PDF for legal use. No entry can be modified or deleted.
